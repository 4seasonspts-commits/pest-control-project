Product Description

ELIMINATOR® 25EC Fipronil is a professional-grade termiticide formulated with Fipronil 25 EC, a non-repellent chemical designed for highly efficient pest management.

Mode of Action (Kaam Karne Ka Tareeqa)

Fipronil acts on the insect nervous system by disrupting nerve signal transmission. Because it is slow-acting and non-repellent, termites unknowingly transfer the chemical back to the colony, achieving total elimination.
